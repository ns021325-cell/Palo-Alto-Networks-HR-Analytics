# Palo-Alto-Networks-HR-Analytics

## Employee Engagement, Satisfaction & Burnout Diagnostic Analysis

This project focuses on analyzing employee engagement, satisfaction, burnout risk and workforce stress factors using HR analytics techniques. The project combines:
    
    • Data preprocessing & normalization
    • Engagement index creation
    • Burnout risk diagnostics
    • Workload & stress analysis
    • Career-stage engagement analysis
    • Workforce KPI generation
    • Interactive dashboard insights

## 📂 Project Objectives

    • Analyze employee engagement and satisfaction levels
    • Identify employees at burnout
    • Measure workload and stress impact on engagement
    • Evaluate career stagnation and disengagement  patterns
    • Compare engagement between retained and attrited employees
    • Develop HR  KPIs for workkforce monitoring


## 🛠 Tools and Skills Used

    • Python
    • Pandas, Numpy
    • Matplotlib,Seaborn, Plotly
    • Plotly
    • Streamlit Application


## 📌 Key Insights
    1. Average Engagement Index stands at 2.72 out of 4.0 indicating moderate but improvable engagement.
    2. Out of 1,470 employees analyzed, 756 are classified as Low burnout risk, 588 as medium and 126 as high burnout risk.
    3. Overtime employees exhibit mixed short-term engagement but significantly elevated long-term turnover risk.
    4. Long commutes and frequent business travel are associated with lower engagement scores.
    5. Stagnation-linked disengagement is most pronounced in mid-level employees(Job-level 2-3) with 6-15 years of tenure.
    6. Employees who left the organization had mean engagement index of 2.513 compared to 2.764 for employees who stayed.
